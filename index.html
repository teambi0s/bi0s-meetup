MEETUP

The aim of this meetup is to bring together cyber security speakers, researchers and enthusiasts to present and share their findings in their respective areas of expertise. The prime focus being cyber security education and awareness, it is community-driven and is always free for both the speakers and the audience. If you are looking for a platform to unveil your explorations, here it is!
